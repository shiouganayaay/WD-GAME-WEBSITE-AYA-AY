About Riot Games

We're a company founded by gamers, for gamers. We make games that we would love to play ourselves.

Our Mission

To be the most player-focused game company in the world. We believe in creating unforgettable gaming experiences and fostering thriving player communities.

Our Values

  • Player Experience First
  • Dare to Dream
  • Thrive Together
  • Execute with Excellence
  • Stay Hungry; Stay Humble

Our Story

Founded in 2006, Riot Games has grown from a small independent game studio to a global leader in game development, publishing, and esports. Our first title, League of Legends, was released in 2009 and has since become one of the most-played PC games in the world. We've since expanded our portfolio with games like VALORANT, Teamfight Tactics, and Legends of Runeterra.

Our Impact

At Riot Games, we believe in pushing the boundaries of gaming. Our games have shaped esports culture and created some of the most dedicated player communities. Beyond games, we’ve pioneered the development of esports leagues, with League of Legends being the foundation of our global esports ecosystem. We aim to elevate the gaming experience through constant innovation, storytelling, and inclusivity.

The Future of Riot Games

Looking forward, Riot Games is focused on expanding our universe, creating new game genres, and reaching more players around the world. As we expand our portfolio, we’re exploring new technologies such as AI, cloud gaming, and VR/AR to take our player experiences to the next level. We continue to nurture the talent within our teams to ensure that every game we create carries the passion and creativity that defines Riot Games.

Meet the Team

Riot Games is made up of some of the brightest minds in the industry. Our team consists of game designers, developers, artists, writers, and esports professionals who are passionate about creating top-tier gaming experiences. Each member of the Riot team brings their own unique talents and perspectives to shape the future of gaming.

Social Impact

We founded Riot with the belief that a company of gamers, deeply are committed to serving the players, so we can deliver a fun and amazing game experiences. We believe that anyone with passion, vision, and perseverance can make a positive impact. As part of that ethos, we also believe that there are ways where we, as a company, can provide long-term value and positive impact to players, partners, Rioters, and the communities we serve.

Sustainability

We want players to be able to enjoy our games for generations to come. To make that future a reality, we have to follow the science to stay up to date on the best practices for sustainability while staying committed to reducing our overall environmental impact. By putting in the work ourselves, working with impactful partners, and sharing best practices with the rest of the gaming industry, we can, and must, push forward towards a healthier world.

Diversity & Inclusion

We strive to cultivate an environment where our lived and learned experiences – identities, perspectives, styles, and crafts – are all leveraged to foster belonging, drive innovation, solve challenges, and enhance the player experience. D&I will always be an important part of shaping the way we show up as a company for players, Rioters, and in communities around the world.
